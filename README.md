# Sales Performance Analysis

### Overview  
An end to end data analytics project using **Python**, **SQL**, and soon **Power BI**, designed to uncover revenue drivers and performance patterns across global sales data.  
The analysis focuses on **regional sales trends**, **product performance**, **deal sizes**, and **customer contribution**, delivering insights to guide data driven business strategies.

---

### Business Problem  
The company relied on intuition for pricing and sales decisions without clear data insights.  
This project aims to:
- Identify which **regions, customers, and product lines** drive the most revenue.  
- Understand how **deal size and discounts** impact profitability.  
- Detect **seasonal patterns** to optimize planning and marketing strategies.

---

### Key Findings
- **Regional Sales:** No significant difference in mean sales across Japan, APAC, and EMEA.  
- **Deal Size:** Medium deals bring the most total revenue; large deals have the highest value per sale.  
- **Product Lines:** Classic Cars dominate revenue but rely on heavy discounts.  
- **Customers:** Top 10 customers contribute roughly to 25% of total revenue.  
- **Seasonality:** Strong Q1 and Q4 peaks which are key months within these quarters for marketing and stock planning.

---

### Business Recommendations
- Reassess **discount strategy** on top selling product lines to protect margins.  
- Focus on **key accounts** (top 20%) with loyalty or retention strategies.  
- Encourage **medium to large deal conversion** through upselling.  
- Align **inventory and promotions** with Q1 or Q4 demand peaks.
